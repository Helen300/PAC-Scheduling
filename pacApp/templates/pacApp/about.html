What is Princeton Dance ?

Weâ€™re a team of dancers aiming to improve the daily experience of the dance community. We built Princeton Dance - a three tier web application for dancers at Princeton University to book and view studio availability for the ten dance studios managed by the Performing Arts Council (PAC) on campus.

Our product integrates with PAC and also features a scheduling component that allows PAC administration to input requests from dance groups for rehearsal spaces and outputs semesterly schedules for dance studios through our algorithm. The output is added to our calendar for all students to view online. Additionally, we provide a Map with detailed information on how to locate the dance studios and an updated list of dance spaces available at the current hour.
